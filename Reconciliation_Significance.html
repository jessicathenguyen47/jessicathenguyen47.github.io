SIGNIFICANCE

Impact

The Uluru Statement From the Heart maintains significance, years after conception. Constitutional reform and the Makaratta Commission persist as bubbling topics of reconciliation in the media. Coronavirus has exposed how vulnerable Indigenous Australians are, which would not be the case with an enshrined voice, say activists. Petitions, marches, organisational support and plans for political change have occurred due to the Statement's influence. These have garnered attention on the issue, and encourage the Australian public to question the Government's current reconciliation measures, practically and symbolically.
