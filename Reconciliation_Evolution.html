EVOLUTION

Timeline

The Uluru Statement From the Heart evolved into a people's fight for healing. Despite the Government's rejection, individuals are still advocating for Voice, Treaty, and Truth today.

7 Dec 2015

Turnbull appoints a Referendum Council.

14 Dec 2016

Referendum Council meets with 12 regional dialogues.

22 May 2017

  • First Nations National Convention begins

  • Draft signed by 250 delegates

26 May 2017

Statement released.

Apr 2017

Activist Thomas Mayor spends 18 months travelling Australia promoting the statement.

30 Jun 2017

Referendum Council gives final report to Turnbull and Shorten.

3 Aug 2017

  • Shorten calls for committee to finalise a referendum question

  • Megan Davis and Pat Anderson disagree, deeming another committee unnecessary

4 Aug 2017

June Oscar urges for constitutional reform: “origins well before the constitution” at Garma Festival.

5 Oct 2017

  • Turnbull presented statement at Garma Festival

  • "Difficult": Undergo careful consideration

  • Pat Anderson: PM lacks commitment - “Only way forward through the people”

26 Oct 2017

  • Turnbull rejects Uluru statement

  • Believes public would vote no in a referendum

  • Criticism ensues

To Present Day

The reconciliation effort continues, with the Statement's healing vision holding strong in the public's eyes and their growing supporters.
